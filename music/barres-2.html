Clamped Bar Modes

The frequency f1 depends upon the density and elasticity of the material from which the bars are made. The amplitudes for the f2 and f3 modes would actually be much smaller than the fundamental. These mode frequencies appear with a tuning fork since it can be considered to be two clamped bars.
