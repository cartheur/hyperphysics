The Euphonium

The euphonium is a member of the tuba family. It has a slightly higher range and more mellow tone.

Euphonium Waveform


Euphonium tone by Wade Bohannon, 5/3/95
 This is a pedal tone of the euphonium. The harmonic spectrum is an interesting example of the nature of the pedal tone. Note that the amount of fundamental is very small, the main energy coming from the upper harmonics which are actual resonances of the air column.
