Magnetic Confinement

Since the magnetic force is always perpendicular to the velocity

a charged particle will be bent into a circular path by the component of magnetic field which is perpendicular to the velocity. Any motion parallel to the magnetic field will be unchanged by it, so the general motion would be a helical motion.
