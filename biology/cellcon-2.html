Cell Theory

It appears that all of life is composed of cells, and this is formalized as the "cell theory" by Karp. The elements of the cell theory are:

  • All organisms are composed of one or more cells.
  • The cell is the structural unit of life.
  • Cells can arise only by division from a preexisting cell.
