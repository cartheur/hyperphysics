Light Sky Under Rainbow

This photo was taken by Ben Lanterman from his back yard. It was taken immediately after a heavy rainstorm when the air was quite saturated. It was at about 4 PM on a summer day. It was taken with a Minolta Maxxum7 camera with lens focal length 28 mm using Fuji 400 film.
