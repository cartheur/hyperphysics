Nuclear Decay

  • The three most common types of nuclear decay (radioactivity): alpha, beta, gamma
  • Other forms of decay include electron capture.
  • Origin of the instability toward decay.
  • Rates of decay stated in terms of half-life.
  • Units and terms to describe radiation dose and exposure.
  • Use of the half-life in radioactive dating.
