Ulexite

This sample of ullmannite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Ullmannite is a sulfide mineral of nickel and antimony with the composition NiSbS. The sample at left is about 3x3 cm and is from Montenarba, Sarrabus, Sardegna, Italy.
