Berthierite

FeSb2S4

This sample of berthierite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Berthierite is an sulfide mineral of antimony and iron with the composition FeSb2S4 . This large sample is about 8x20 cm and is from Chisbaya Maria, Herja, Transylvania, Romania.
