Connellite

Cu19Cl4SO4(OH)32.3H2O

This sample of connellite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Connellite is a sulfate mineral of copper and chlorine with the composition Cu19Cl4SO4(OH)32.3H2O. The sample at left is about 10 cm across and is from Bisbee, Arizona.
