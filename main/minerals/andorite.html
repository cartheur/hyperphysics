Andorite

AgPbSb3S6

This sample of andorite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Andorite is a sulfide mineral of silver, lead and antimony with the composition AgPbSb3S6 . This sample is about 8x7 cm and is from Oruro, Bolivia.
