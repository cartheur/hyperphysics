Montroydite

HgO

This sample of montroydite formed with calcite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Montroydite is an oxide mineral of mercury with the composition HgO . This sample is about 9x7 cm and is from Terlingua, Texas.

Montroydite often shows a deep red color.
