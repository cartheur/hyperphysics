Zinkenite

This sample of zinkenite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Zinkenite is an sulfide mineral of lead and antimony with the composition Pb9Sb22S42 . This sample is about 6x6 cm and is from Huanchaca, Potosi, Bolivia.
