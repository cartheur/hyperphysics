Awaruite

Ni3Fe

These samples of awaruite are displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Awaruite is a compound of nickel and iron with the composition Ni3Fe. The sample at left is about 3x3 cm and the one at right is about 3x1.5 cm. They are from Smith River, California.
