Hedenbergite

CaFeSi2O6

Hedenbergite is a silicate of calcium and iron with composition CaFeSi2O6. This sample is about 20x15 cm and is from Nordmark,Sweden. It is on display at the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History.

The small sample shown to the right is aegirine.
