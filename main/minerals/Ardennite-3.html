Ardennite

Mg1+xMn4Al5-x(AsO4)(SiO4)2(Si3O10)(OH)6

This sample of ardennite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Ardennite is a silicate of magnesium and manganese. It also contains arsenic with the composition Mg1+xMn4Al5-x(AsO4)(SiO4)2(Si3O10)(OH)6. The sample at left is about 8 cm across and is from Ottre, Ardennes, Belgium.
