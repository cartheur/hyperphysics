Linnaeite

This sample of linnaeite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Linnaeite is a sulfide mineral of cobalt with the composition Co3S4 . This sample is about 9x7 cm and is from Littfeld, Nord Rhein-Westphalen, Germany.
