Stibarsen

This sample of stibarsen is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Stibarsen is a mineral of the compound SbAs of antimony and arsenic. The sample at left is about 12x10 cm and is from Rio Moctezuma, Sonora, Mexico.
