Romanechite

This sample of romanechite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Romanechite is an oxide of barium and manganese with the composition (Ba, H2O)2Mn5O10 . This sample is about 9x7 cm and is from Marquette County, Michigan.
