Glaucodot

(Co,Fe)AsS

This sample of glaucodot is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Glaucodot is a sulfide mineral of cobalt, iron and arsenic with the composition (Co,Fe)AsS. The sample is about 4x4 cm and is from Tunaberg, Sweden.
