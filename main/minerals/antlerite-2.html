Antlerite

Cu3SO4(OH)4

This sample of antlerite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Antlerite is a sulfate mineral of copper with the composition Cu3SO4(OH)4. The sample at left is about 30 cm across and is from Chuquicamata, Atacama, Chile.
