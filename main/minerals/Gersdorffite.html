Gersdorffite

NiAsS

This sample of gersdorffite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Gersdorffite is an sulfide mineral of nickel and arsenic with the composition NiAsS . This sample is about 20x20 cm and is from Bou Azzer, Morocco.
