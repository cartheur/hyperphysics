Devilline

CaCu4(SO4)2(OH)6.3H2O

This sample of devilline is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Devilline is a sulfate mineral of copper with the composition CaCu4(SO4)2(OH)6.3H2O. The larger sample at left is about 9 cm across and is from Spana Dolina, Slovenko, Slovakia.
