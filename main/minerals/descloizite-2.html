Descloizite

PbZn(VO)4(OH)

This sample of descloizite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Descloizite is an oxide mineral of lead, zinc vanadium with the composition PbZn(VO)4(OH). The sample at left is about 10 cm across and is from Grootfontein, Namibia.
