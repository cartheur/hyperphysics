Wurtzite

This sample of wurtzite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Wurtzite is a sulfide mineral of zinc and iron with the composition (Zn,Fe)S. The sample is about 4x4 cm and is from Animas section, Llallagua, Potosi, Bolivia.
