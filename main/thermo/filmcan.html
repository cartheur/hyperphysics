Exploding Film Can

A cold explosion can be produced by placing liquid nitrogen in a film can. Though caution is advised, it seems like harmless fun. The Fuji type film can shown builds up more pressure than the black Kodak can, though both are interesting. The top of the film can hits the ceiling forcefully.
