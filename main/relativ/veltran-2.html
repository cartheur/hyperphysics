Relativistic Velocity Transformation

No two objects can have a relative velocity greater than c! But what if I observe a spacecraft traveling at 0.8c and it fires a projectile which it observes to be moving at 0.7c with respect to it!? Velocities must transform according to the Lorentz transformation, and that leads to a very non-intuitive result called Einstein velocity addition.
