The Cornu Spiral

The Cornu spiral is a graphical aid for evaluating the Fresnel integrals which show up in the evaluation of the diffraction intensities for the Fresnel diffraction of the light from a slit. It lends itself to the calculation of diffraction from slits, barriers, and opaque strips.
