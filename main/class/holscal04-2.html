Hologram Scaling, Physics 3901, Fall 04

This year's project involved using a Nikon D70 digital camera to record images of the holographic images.

The above image was taken through a transmission hologram. The hologram was illuminated from behind by a helium-neon laser which has been passed through a diverging lens to spread the beam over the hologram.

The images above were taken from left, center and right positions, showing the parallax of the images.

Experimenters: Amber Bishop, Ryan Hennings, Morgan Callender, Matt Davis, Leigh Buholtz
