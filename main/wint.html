Work done by a variable force

 The basic work relationship W=Fx is a special case which applies only to constant force along a straight line. In the more general case of a force which changes with distance, the work may still be calculated as the area under the curve. Work:General Definition

The general definition of work done by a force must take into account the fact that the force may vary in both magnitude and direction, and that the path followed may also change in direction. All these things can be taken into account by defining work as an integral. 		Index
