Particle in Finite-Walled Box

For the finite potential well, the solution to the Schrodinger equation gives a wavefunction with an exponentially decaying penetration into the classicallly forbidden region.

Confining a particle to a smaller space requires a larger confinement energy. Since the wavefunction penetration effectively "enlarges the box", the finite well energy levels are lower than those for the infinite well.
