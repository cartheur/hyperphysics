Parity and the Particle in a Box

In the one-dimensional case, parity refers to the "evenness" or "oddness" of a function with respect to reflection about x=0.

The particle in a box problem can give some insight into the importance of parity in quantum mechanics. The box has a line of symmetry down the center of the box (x=0).
