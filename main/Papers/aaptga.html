Who is using HyperPhysics?

Informal statistics from the server log indicate about 15 hits per user on average, so 30 million hits translates to roughly 2 million users.

Email traffic has increased proportionately, and an informal tally of the emails indicates the following frequency of users.

  • Students.
  • High school teachers.
  • Engineering and technical personnel in industry.
  • Independent learners.

Search engines give over 2000 links to HyperPhysics, mostly from educators or educational institutions.
