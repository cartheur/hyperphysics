Hubble Telescope Repair

The optical problems of the Hubble Space Telescope were corrected by a shuttle mission in December 1993. This put it back in service in time to image the impacts on Jupiter by comet Shoemaker-Levy 9. In early 1997 there was a second servicing mission.
