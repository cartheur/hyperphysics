Meta-autunite

Ca(UO2)2(PO4)2.6H2O

This sample of meta-autunite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Meta-autunite is a phosphate mineral of uranium with the composition Ca(UO2)2(PO4)2.6H2O. The sample at left is about 12 cm across and is from Central Massif, France.

This sample of meta-autunite is about 20 cm across and is from Daybreak mine, Spokane, Washington.
