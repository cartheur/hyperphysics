Miargyrite

AgSbS2

This sample of miargyrite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Miargyrite is an sulfide mineral of antimony and silver with the composition AgSbS2 . This sample is about 6x6 cm and is from Baia Sprie, Crisana-Maramures, Romania.
