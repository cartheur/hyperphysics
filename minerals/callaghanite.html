Callaghanite

Cu2Mg2CO3(OH)6.2H2O

This sample of Callaghanite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Callaghanite is a carbonate mineral which contains copper and magnesium with the composition Cu2Mg2CO3(OH)6.2H2O. This sample is about 18x10 cm and is from Brucite quarry, Gabbs, Nevada.
