Stephanite

This sample of Stephanite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Stephanite is an sulfide mineral of silver and antimony with the composition Ag5SbS4 . This sample is about 3x7 cm and is from Husky mine, Elsa, Yukon Territory, Canada.
