Bannisterite

KCaMn21(Si,Al)32O76(OH)1612H2O

These samples of bannisterite are displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Bannisterite contains manganese and has the composition KCaMn21(Si,Al)32O76(OH)1612H2O. This sample is about 11 cm wide and is from Franklin, New Jersey.

This sample of bannisterite is about 5 cm long.
