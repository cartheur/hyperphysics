Senaite

This sample of senaite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Senaite is an oxide mineral of lead, titanium, manganese, and iron with the composition Pb(Ti, Fe, Mn)21O38 . This sample is about 9x9 cm and is from Diamantina, Minas Gerais, Brazil.
