Cumengite

Pb21Cu20Cl42(OH)40.6H2O

This sample of cumengite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Cumengite is a chloride of copper and lead with the composition Pb21Cu20Cl42(OH)40.6H2O. The large sample at left is about 12 cm across. This mineral been also labeled cumengeite.
