Weloganite

This small sample of Weloganite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Weloganite is a carbonate mineral which contains strontium and zirconium with the composition Na2(Sr,Ca)3Zr(CO3)6.3H2O. This sample is about 1.5x2 cm and originated in Francon Quarry, Montreal, Quebec, Canada.
