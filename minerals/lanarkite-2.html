Lanarkite

Pb2O(SO4)

This sample of lanarkite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Lanarkite is a sulfate mineral of lead with the composition Pb2O(SO4). The sample at left is about 8 cm across and is from Leadhills, Strathclyde, Scotland.
