Paramelaconite

Cu4O3

This sample of paramelaconite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Paramelaconite is an oxide mineral of copper with the composition Cu4O3. The sample at left is about 10 cm across and is from Bisbee, Arizona.
