Clinoclase

Cu3AsO4(OH)3

This sample of clinoclase is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Clinoclase is an arsenate mineral of copper with the composition Cu3AsO4(OH)3. The sample at left is about 8 cm across and is from Tintic district, Utah.
