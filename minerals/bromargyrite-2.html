Bromargyrite

AgBr

This sample of bromargyrite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Bromargyrite is a compound of silver with bromine, AgBr. The sample at left is about 14 cm across and is from Broken Hill, New South Wales, Australia.
