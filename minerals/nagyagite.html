Nagyagite

Au(Pb,Sb,Fe)8(Te,S)11

This sample of nagyagite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Nagyagite is an sulfide mineral of gold,lead, antimony, iron and tellurium with the composition Au(Pb,Sb,Fe)8(Te,S)11. This sample is about 5x5 cm and is from Sacaramb, Tranyslvania, Romania.
