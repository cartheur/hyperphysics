Diaboleite

Cu3Pb2Cl2(OH)4

This sample of diaboleite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Diaboleite is a chloride of copper and lead with the composition Cu3Pb2Cl2(OH)4. The sample at left is about 3 cm across and is from Mammoth mine, Tiger, Arizona.
