Reinerite

This sample of reinerite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Reinerite contains zinc and arsenic with the composition Zn3(AsO3)2. This sample is about 6x5 cm and is from Tsumeb, Namibia. The sample also contains smithsonite and tennantite.
