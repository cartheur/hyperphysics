Artinite

Mg2CO3(OH)2.3H2O

This sample of Artinite is displayed in the Smithsonian Museum of Natural History. Artinite is a carbonate mineral of magnesium with the composition Mg2CO3(OH)2.3H2O . This sample is about 15x15 cm and is from San Benito County, California.
